\caption[Two-stream lead-sheet recognition.]{%
  The two-stream design: two CRNN--CTC recognisers of the same
  architectural family (\Cref{fig:soa-crnn-pipeline}) but with
  disjoint vocabularies, disjoint training corpora, and no shared
  parameters or cross-attention.  The music stream emits LMX tokens
  over a vocabulary of $\approx 80$ LMX symbols; the chord stream emits
  jazz-chord strings over a $29$-character alphabet.  Each is
  pre-trained on its own synthetic corpus and fine-tuned independently
  on a small hand-labelled real set.  Merging into the JSON output
  happens in post-processing, not inside the model.}
\label{fig:two-stream-arch}
